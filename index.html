Kaunto

Kaunto was a web project made with Mongo and Nodejs with Expressjs. The problem was managers at the store spent too much time counting safe at the end of the night. This was because of all the recording and calculations that needed to be done by hand. Kaunto accepts a log in by a manager and once the number of rolls of certain coins is inputed the calculations are done automatically and saved to a database.
